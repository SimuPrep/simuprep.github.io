About

Solving the confidence gap in professional training

Educational institutions and corporations spend millions on hyper-realistic simulations, yet many students enter these high-stakes environments underprepared. The simulation doesn't fail — the preparation does.

SimuPrep provides the essential pre-simulation layer. By leveraging top-of-the-line OpenAI models, we provide students with tailored scenarios and MCQs that adapt to their specific performance. Our platform doesn't just test knowledge — it builds the psychological readiness and technical confidence required to succeed in high-pressure environments.

How it works

From onboarding to simulation-ready in three steps

01

Identify the readiness gap

SimuPrep assesses each learner's baseline knowledge and surfaces the exact cognitive and psychological gaps that could compromise their simulation performance.

02

Train with adaptive AI scenarios

Scenarios and MCQs powered by top-tier OpenAI models adapt in real time to each learner's specific performance — closing gaps where it matters most before simulation day.

03

Enter the simulation ready

Learners arrive with verified technical competence and the psychological resilience to perform under pressure. Instructors see cohort readiness before the session begins.
